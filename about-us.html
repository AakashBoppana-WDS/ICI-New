About Us

GAYATRI VIDYA PARISHAD COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING (Autonomous), Visakhapatnam

 The GVP College of Engineering, Visakhapatnam started in 1996, has very quickly established itself as one of the most-preferred private engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh. College has been accredited by NAAC in 2009 with an A grade (with a score of 3.47/4.00). The college has been sanctioned “Autonomous” status by the UGC and conferred by the affiliating University, JNT University, Kakinada in 2009.

ICI bridges

ICI bridges the gap between academic learning and industry practices in concrete technology. With 310+ student chapters across India, it provides hands-on exposure to the latest advancements in construction.

What We Do

Organize workshops, seminars, and industry visits.

Provide training, internships, and networking opportunities.

Enhance technical skills through expert lectures and competitions.

Support research and innovation in sustainable construction.

By being a part of ICI, students gain practical knowledge, industry connections, and career opportunities, shaping them into future leaders in civil engineering.
